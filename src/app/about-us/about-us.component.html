Your Story of Financial Freedom

MoneySaga Consultancy Services (MCS) is an Indian Company situated in Pune, Maharashtra. MCS is better known for its Financial Education and business into the Financial Markets.

With 6 different courses in the field of Finance has emerged with a well-known brand in Education and also in helping people with Financial and Non- Financial background to Earn its 2nd Income and be ready to beat the inflation and any other calamities, crises or situations like pandemics.

Pravin Suryawanshi

MD & CEO of MoneySaga Consultancy

With a Dynamic Vision and Energy, Mr. Pravin N Suryawanshi Started MoneySaga Consultancy Services. Being a True Indian and a son of a Farmer and coming from a poor and humble family background, has lot of respect for the Hard-workers and Unemployed people and the most for the Soldiers and Farmers of India.

After Being Financially Free since last 6 years now his only Motto is to Serve the Nation by providing money making skills income source to all the Unemployed and a Second income for others so that they can also achieve Early Financial Freedom.

His Vision is to reach every village, town, city where mobile network can reach and Educate at least One member of Every Family to become Financially Free so that He / She would educate the other members of the Family to achieve their Financial Freedom.

As MD of MoneySaga company he wants to provide valuable and Life changing Education to our clients in various age group starting from Teenagers to the Senior Citizens and from School going Children, Collegians, Employees, Businessmen, House Wives and all those who love making and multiplying Money.
